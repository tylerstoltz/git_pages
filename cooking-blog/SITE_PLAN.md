# islacooks.com Build Plan

## Phase 1: Foundation (Now)
- Establish core IA: home, recipe template, category hubs, and search.
- Lock global components: header/footer, recipe card patterns, ad slots, and SEO schema.
- Create asset workflow for hero + card images (SVG placeholders replaced by PNGs).

## Phase 2: Content Expansion
- Publish 10-15 cornerstone recipes across dinner, seafood, and meal-prep categories.
- Add internal linking between recipe cards, category hubs, and related recipes.
- Build a recipe index page with filters (time, protein, diet).

## Phase 3: Monetization & Growth
- Add affiliate kit pages for staple tools and ingredients.
- Insert ad slots across recipe pages (in-content, sidebar, below recipe card).
- Add email capture upgrades (meal plan, grocery list, printable PDF).

## Phase 4: Optimization
- Improve page speed with image compression and lazy loading.
- Expand FAQ + cooking tips to capture long-tail SEO queries.
- Track analytics and refine top-performing recipes with A/B title tests.
