About UUIDs

  • UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) is a 128-bit identifier
  • Version 4 UUIDs are randomly generated (used here)
  • Format: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx (where x is hexadecimal, y is 8, 9, A, or B)
  • Collision Probability: Extremely low (~1 in 2^122)
  • Use Cases: Database keys, session IDs, distributed systems, unique identifiers
